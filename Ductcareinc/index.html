Duct Cleaning Services with DuctsCare

DuctsCare specializes in providing complete duct cleaning solutions for ducts and exhausts. We provide advanced cleaning services for all types of ducts and exhausts regardless of their size, form and dimension. Our expertise lies in HVAC inspection, maintenance, cleaning and restoration of residential as well as commercial air ducts and dryer vents. Indoor air can be up to 70 times more polluted than outdoor air. At DuctsCare, our top priority is to improve and maintain the quality of indoor air so that you breathe fresh and odourless air all throughout the day. We make use of high quality and well-maintained equipment that ensures our perfectly delivered services leave you satisfied.

5 Signs You Should Have Your Ducts Cleaned

If you see these signs, it’s probably the right time to call us.

You haven’t changed the air filter on time

The registers and vent covers have visible dust

You see mould in or around your HVAC system

Airflow isn’t consistent from room to room

You can hear noises in the duct work
